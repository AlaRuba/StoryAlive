Milestone 5: Functional Prototype II


Progress since last milestone

Since our user testing session last week, we decided to implement new features and refine existing ones based on the feedback we received. This week, we hope to test our product again with children at the local Palo Alto Children's Library, since it is difficult to schedule time with kids during the class day at elementary schools.

Features of latest prototype

  • More audio recordings to guide user through activity pages, i.e. "Drag the ring onto me!"
  • 2 main activity pages have bigger and clear objects and shelves
  • 2 prompt pages to ask user "What do you think will happen next?" before suspenseful parts of story
  • Refined swipe gesture to make using it easier, more intuitive, and effective
  • Animations of various illustrations in the story to make reading experience feel more alive
  • Page 8-9 Drag 1 cursor to Franny's butt and transition to fart scene on page 10-11

Changes we're currently working on

  • Make glowing sphere as cursor
  • Designing more interactive/game-like pages for collaboration
    • Pages 8-9: Two kids drag their cursors (glowing sphere) to Franny’s butt → automatic transition to the next page (fart page).
    • Pages 26-27: One kid rubs cricket (circle gesture?), one kid causes Franny to dance
    • Ability to draw out light of a firefly
